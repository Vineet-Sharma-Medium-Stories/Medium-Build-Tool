## 📖 Introduction

**Welcome to the complete story catalog of the 2026 AI Metromap.**

Think of this as your subway map's legend—the guide that shows you every station, every line, and every connection before you start your journey. Unlike a traditional curriculum that forces you down a predetermined path, this catalog lets you choose your own route based on your goals, background, and interests.

**How to Use This Catalog:**

- **Start with the Master Story Arc** – These four stories form the foundation of the Metromap philosophy. Read them first to understand the "why" behind the structure.

- **Choose Your Express Line** – After the Master Arc, pick one Series that matches your goals. Want to build LLM applications? Start with Series C. Focused on production ML? Series D is your track.

- **Transfer Between Lines** – Each story includes "connections" to related stations. The catalog shows you these relationships through parent links.

- **Go Deep Where It Matters** – You don't need to read every story. Master one line deeply, then expand to others when needed.

**What Makes This Different:**

Traditional AI education is a highway—you start at mile 0 and drive straight until you reach the destination. If you miss an exit, you're lost.

The Metromap is a transit system. You:
- Start at **Foundations Station** (the central hub)
- Choose an **express line** that excites you (LLMs, Vision, Production)
- Make **transfers** to other lines when your needs change
- Get off at **domain stations** to build real-world applications

This catalog is your map. Let's explore it together.
