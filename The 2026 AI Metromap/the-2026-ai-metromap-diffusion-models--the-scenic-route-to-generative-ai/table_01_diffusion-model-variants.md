# ## 📊 Diffusion Model Variants

| Model | Year | Key Innovation | Use Case |
|-------|------|----------------|----------|
| DDPM | 2020 | Original diffusion model | Image generation |
| DDIM | 2021 | Faster sampling (fewer steps) | Efficient generation |
| Stable Diffusion | 2022 | Latent space diffusion | Text-to-image, open source |
| DALL-E 2 | 2022 | CLIP + Diffusion | High-quality text-to-image |
| Imagen | 2022 | Text-only conditioning | Photorealistic generation |
| Midjourney | 2022 | Style-focused training | Artistic generation |
| Sora | 2024 | Video diffusion | Text-to-video |
