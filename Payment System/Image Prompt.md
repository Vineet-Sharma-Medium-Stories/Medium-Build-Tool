## Tips for Best Results

1. **Specify aspect ratio**: 1920x1080 or 16:9 works best for Medium
2. **Mention background**: Light theme ensures readability for text overlays
3. **Include specific logos**: Names like VisaNet, Mastercard, RuPay help the AI recognize correct branding
4. **Define hierarchy**: "Prominent .NET logo" vs "small author credit"
5. **Suggest color palette**: Helps maintain professional consistency
6. **Add technical elements**: "Binary pattern", "connector lines", "data flow" create authentic technical aesthetic
