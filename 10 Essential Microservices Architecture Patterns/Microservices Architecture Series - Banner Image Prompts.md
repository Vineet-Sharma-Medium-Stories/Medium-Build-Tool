## Usage Instructions

These banner prompts are optimized for:

1. **DALL-E 3** - Use the detailed prompts as-is
2. **Midjourney** - Add `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6` at the end
3. **Stable Diffusion** - Use as positive prompt with appropriate negative prompts

**Recommended Negative Prompts:**
```
dark background, neon colors, harsh shadows, grunge, vintage, sepia, dark mode, black background, high contrast, oversaturated, comic style, anime, 3D render, photorealistic people, text with wrong spelling, cluttered, noisy
```

**For Consistent Results:**
- Maintain the color hex codes
- Keep the signature description consistent
- Ensure all technology logos are recognizable but not overpowering
- The banners should look professional and suitable for technical article headers
